What's needed to move forward with SalesForce?

Fix Duplicate Importing

  1. Admin access to SalesForce is needed by the email team in order to create Workflow Rules. Access to this functionality will be needed for future imports.
  2. An individual will be necessary to troubleshoot all issues with importing records into SalesForce.

Subscriptions Form

  1. Email Subscription Form needs to feed from our Website to Salesforce
    1. Can we just re-skin the Profile Center?
    2. How do Leads/Contacts “log into” profile center?
    3. If a user enters their email address into our email form can their subscriptions (emails they've subscribed to) automatically populate in real-time.

Clean Subscriptions List

  1. The extent of cleaning the list is almost entirely dependent on how duplicate importing is fixed.
  2. Audit of SalesForce field formats and application of the updated formats to the fields in the subscriptions list (known fields with inconsistencies: country, phone number, name, etc).
  3. Have someone determine whether the members of the subscriptions email list are leads, contacts, opportunities, etc.
